A Good Question

After I presented the new game mechanic features we have in the pipeline for the Qt Developer Network during our project fair on Monday, I received a good question. I had mentioned that the principles we apply mostly cater to men.

So, why did you give up on the ladies?

Well, I didn’t. Thing is however that “social” does not necessarily mean the same for everybody. Prior to coming up with the specifications for the latest reward/reputation features, I did extensive research. And I have a close eye on our site obviously. What I have learned now is that although some things work equally well for both genders, others don’t. Surprise? Not really. Let me give you an example. One of my favourite community sites is Ravelry. It is built around knitting and crocheting topics and it’s huge! It has plenty of great features but the one I’m trying to make a point about here are the groups.
